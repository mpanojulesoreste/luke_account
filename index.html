My Purpose in Writing

Through my observations and interviews with eyewitnesses, I have documented not only the life and teachings of Jesus of Nazareth but also witnessed a revolutionary model of leadership that transformed ordinary people into world-changers.

Key Themes in My Account

Vision & Mission

Jesus began with a clear mission to bring good news to the poor, proclaim release to captives, and let the oppressed go free.

Team Selection

He carefully selected his disciples, looking for those with ambition and values aligned with his cause.

Servant Leadership

Jesus led by example, consistently modeling the behaviors he expected from his followers.

Training & Development

He invested time in training his disciples, preparing them to continue his work after his departure.

Correction & Growth

Jesus corrected his disciples' misconceptions and redirected their focus to the mission.

Recognition

He celebrated their achievements and recognized their potential to transform the world.

"The team that Jesus made changed the world. From Jerusalem to the ends of the earth, they spread his message, implementing a model of transformative leadership that continues to influence humanity to this day."
